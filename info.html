Model United Nations Conference

About the Conference

The Model United Nations (MUN) Conference is a prestigious and educational event where students simulate the workings of the United Nations. Participants act as delegates representing different countries and engage in debates and discussions on various global issues.

MUN conferences provide an excellent platform for students to develop their diplomatic and public speaking skills while gaining a deeper understanding of international affairs and global challenges.

Conference Schedule

The MUN Conference will be held on October 15-17, 2023 at the Convention Center in the heart of the city. The conference will span three days, during which delegates will participate in various committee sessions and engage in lively debates.

The conference will cover a wide range of topics, including climate change, human rights, global security, and sustainable development goals, among others.
