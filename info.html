NITS Model United Nations Conference

About the Conference

The NITSMUN Parliamentary Conference is a prestigious and educational event where students simulate the workings of the Indian Parliament. Participants act as delegates representing different parties and engage in debates and discussions on various national and global issues.

These conferences provide an excellent platform for students to develop their diplomatic and public speaking skills while gaining a deeper understanding of international affairs and global challenges.

Conference Schedule

The NITSMUN Conference will be held on October 15-17, 2023 at the Convention Center in NIT Silchar. The conference will span three days, during which delegates will participate in various committee sessions and engage in lively debates.

The conference will cover a wide range of topics, including climate change, human rights, global security, and sustainable development goals, among others.
